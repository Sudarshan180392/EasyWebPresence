Privacy Policy

Easy Web Presence  ·  easywebpresence.co.in  ·  Effective Date: March 7, 2026

Welcome to Easy Web Presence. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at easywebpresence.co.in, engage with our services, or communicate with us. Please read this notice carefully — it will help you understand what we do with the information we collect.

1. Introduction

EasyWeb Presence ("we," "our," or "us") operates as an independent web design and development service based in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. We are committed to handling all personal information shared with us responsibly, transparently, and in accordance with applicable Indian laws and internationally recognised privacy principles.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you express an interest in our services or contact us directly.

  1. a.Personal Information Provided by You: We may collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, billing information, and project-related files or credentials necessary to complete our web development services.
  2. b.Automatically Collected Information: When you visit or navigate our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information that does not directly identify you — such as your IP address, browser type, device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, and general location data.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following legitimate business purposes:

  1. a.To fulfil and manage your requests: To deliver the web design, frontend, and backend development services you have contracted us for.
  2. b.To facilitate account or project setup: If you choose to link third-party accounts (such as Google or GitHub) to assist with project delivery, we use only the information necessary to carry out that specific service.
  3. c.To send administrative communications: We may use your contact information to inform you about service updates, changes to our policies, or project-related matters.
  4. d.To request feedback: We may contact you after project delivery to request feedback about your experience with our services.
  5. e.To respond to enquiries and provide support: We use your information to respond to your messages, resolve issues, and provide assistance related to your project or our services.
4. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

  1. a.Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party vendors or contractors who assist in delivering our services — such as payment processors, web hosting providers, and domain registrars — strictly for the purposes of project execution.
  2. b.Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information where legally required to do so — for example, in response to a court order, government request, or applicable Indian law.
  3. c.With Your Consent: We will share your information with any other party only upon obtaining your explicit consent.
5. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law (such as tax, accounting, or record-keeping obligations). When your information is no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymise it.

6. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We have implemented appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect the personal information we process. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we take every reasonable precaution to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security against all possible threats.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  1. a.Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  2. b.Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  3. c.Request deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via WhatsApp or email using the details provided at the bottom of this page.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or operational updates. The updated version will be indicated by a revised "Effective Date" at the top of this page and will take effect as soon as it is published. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. Limitation of Liability & Third-Party Disclaimer

EasyWeb Presence acts solely as a technical developer and independent service provider. We design and build website infrastructure on behalf of our clients. Once a completed website has been handed over to the client, EasyWeb Presence does not manage, control, monitor, or assume any liability for the end-user data collected, stored, or processed through that website.

Furthermore, EasyWeb Presence shall not be held liable for any data breaches, leaks, unauthorised access, or loss of information arising from:

  1. a.Vulnerabilities in third-party hosting servers, server infrastructure, or cloud platforms.
  2. b.Failures, breaches, or security incidents involving third-party payment gateways, external APIs, or integrated services.
  3. c.Unauthorised access to the client's systems, accounts, credentials, or hosting environment after project handover.
  4. d.Any data processing, storage, or transmission activities conducted on the final deployed website that are outside the scope of EasyWeb Presence's contracted development work.

By engaging our services and accepting project delivery, the client agrees to indemnify and hold EasyWeb Presence, its developer, and any associated personnel harmless from any legal claims, regulatory actions, financial losses, or damages arising from data processing activities conducted on the final deployed website — including but not limited to claims under the Information Technology Act, 2000, and any applicable data protection regulations in India.
