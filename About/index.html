Our Story

Easy Web Presence was founded with a clear mission — to give professionals and business owners a website that actually works. Not a rented template. Not a subscription trap. A real, owned, performant digital asset.

Based in India, we code every website from scratch using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, worldwide. No WordPress. No page builders. No borrowed themes. Just clean, precise code that loads fast, stays secure, and belongs entirely to you.

Every client works directly with the developer — not a sales team, not a support ticket queue. You get honest communication, faster delivery, and a website built around your real business needs.

We currently operate as an independent development studio with a long-term vision to grow into a fully registered entity — while staying true to our core promise: transparent pricing, clean code, and no surprises.

Where We're Headed
