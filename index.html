The Technical Truth

Why We Don't Use WordPress or Page Builders

We code every website in pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Here is exactly why that choice protects you.

⚡

Lightning-Fast Speed

WordPress relies on heavy themes, databases, and bloated plugins that slow your site considerably. Custom HTML/CSS/JS sites are feather-light, loading instantly — keeping visitors engaged and significantly improving your SEO rankings.

🛡️

Bulletproof Security

WordPress is a constant target for hackers due to its widespread use. Vulnerabilities in third-party plugins pose serious risks. A custom static site has no database and no plugins — making it virtually immune to the most common web attacks.

🔧

Zero Maintenance Headaches

WordPress requires constant updates to core software, themes, and plugins. An update can break your site overnight. Custom code simply works — once deployed, it requires zero forced updates to stay secure and functional.

🎨

100% Customisation

Page builders force you to work within template limitations. By writing from scratch, we have absolute control over every single pixel and interaction — ensuring your website looks and functions exactly as your business needs, with zero compromises.
